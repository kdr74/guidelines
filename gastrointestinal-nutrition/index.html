ICU Nutrition Guidelines

UHBW Critical Care | Interactive Clinical Guideline

Comprehensive evidence-based nutrition guideline for critically ill adult patients, covering assessment, delivery methods, monitoring, and management of complications.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Nutritional assessment and screening (NUTRIC score)
  • Energy and protein requirements calculation
  • Route selection: enteral vs parenteral nutrition
  • Enteral feeding: NG, OG, NJ, OJ tube feeding protocols
  • Parenteral nutrition: indications and monitoring
  • Feeding in specific conditions: sepsis, renal failure, liver failure, pancreatitis
  • Management of feed intolerance and gastric residual volumes
  • Refeeding syndrome: recognition, prevention, and management
  • Micronutrient supplementation protocols
  • Immune-modulating nutrition (glutamine, omega-3, arginine)

Nutrition Calculator

Interactive Clinical Decision Support Tool

Advanced nutrition calculator for critically ill patients with comprehensive metabolic tracking, multi-day cumulative analysis, and automated nutritional adequacy assessment.

Interactive Features:

  • Patient Parameters: Weight, height, BMI, ideal body weight calculations
  • Energy Requirements: Multiple calculation methods (25-30 kcal/kg, Penn State, indirect calorimetry targets)
  • Protein Requirements: Adjusted for obesity, renal failure, liver disease, and critical illness
  • Daily Intake Tracking: Separate inputs for enteral, parenteral, and propofol nutrition
  • Cumulative Analysis: Multi-day tracking with running totals and averages
  • Metabolic State Tracking: Monitor fed/fasted states, feeding interruptions, and reasons
  • Adequacy Assessment: Automatic calculation of % energy and protein targets achieved
  • Visual Analytics: Graphs showing daily intake trends and cumulative adequacy
  • Refeeding Risk: Automated risk assessment and phosphate monitoring recommendations
  • Feed Prescription: Generates specific feeding regimen recommendations

Advanced Capabilities:

  • Multi-source nutrition tracking (EN + PN + propofol combined)
  • Day-by-day cumulative totals with running averages
  • Metabolic state monitoring (fed/fasted/transitioning)
  • Feeding interruption logging with categorized reasons
  • Automated protein adequacy alerts
  • Obesity-adjusted calculations (using ideal body weight)
  • Renal and hepatic failure dose adjustments
  • Export capability for documentation and audit
