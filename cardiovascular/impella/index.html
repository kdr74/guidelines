About This Guideline

Authors: M Govier (ICU Consultant), T Johnson (Cardiology Consultant), M Mariathas (Cardiology Consultant), J Strange (Cardiology Consultant)

For Staff: All staff managing patients with Impella devices

Patients: Adult patients requiring mechanical circulatory support during high-risk primary percutaneous coronary intervention (PPCI), or for cardiogenic shock

This guideline provides comprehensive clinical protocols for the management of patients with Impella CP with SmartAssist mechanical circulatory support devices in the Intensive Care Unit setting.
