Section 5: Nursing Management

Version 3.2 | March 2025 – December 2026

Specific Nursing Management

Hourly Monitoring

  • Impella parameters: Flow rates, purge pressure, purge fluid rate and placement signal from the AIC screen
    • Add the CIS "Impella" row group on Phillips ICCA
  • Limb assessment: Monitoring of pedal pulses manually and with Doppler
  • Access site: Assessment for haematoma and bleeding

4-Hourly Monitoring

  • Impella position on catheter shaft
  • Impella position should also be checked using the AIC waveform screen after each re-positioning of patient

Patient Positioning

  • Do not raise the head of the bed to higher than a 30-degree angle
  • To prevent the purge tubing from kinking, do not allow the red Impella plug to hang freely from the catheter and do not bend the catheter near the red Impella plug

Safety Checks at Start of Each Shift

✓ Shift Safety Checklist

  • Impella console plugged into mains and the UPS plug
  • Backup console available in cath lab
  • Impella cart in secure place and brake is engaged
  • Ensure Tuohy-Borst adapter is tight and secure
