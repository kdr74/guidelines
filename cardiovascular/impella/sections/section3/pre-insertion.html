Pre-Insertion Considerations

⚠️ Critical Requirement

All insertions of Impella catheters must be performed in the cardiac catheter laboratory or cardiac theatres by a trained consultant Interventional Cardiologist or Cardiac Surgeon.

Pre-Insertion Checklist

  1. Peripheral Vascular Disease (PVD) Assessment

    All patients planned electively for an Impella (either as an inpatient or outpatient) should be assessed for a history of peripheral vascular disease.

    • If history of PVD or claudication: obtain CT aortic/iliac/femoral angiogram prior to planned device insertion
    • Assess patency of peripheral vessels
    • Plan appropriate insertion approach:
      • Femoral (standard)
      • Subclavian (percutaneous or surgical)
      • Axillary (percutaneous or surgical)
  2. Blood Tests Required

    All patients must have the following blood tests prior to the procedure:

    • Full blood count
    • Coagulation screen
    • Renal biochemistry
    • Two sets of group and save
  3. Baseline Echocardiography

    Baseline echo required to ensure there is:

    • No significant aortic valve disease
    • No LV thrombus
  4. Patient Admission

    If the patient is not a current inpatient, admit to a cardiac ward/CCU the day before the procedure.

  5. Anaesthetic Support Planning

    Strong consideration needs to be given to having anaesthetic support planned and available from the start of the case, given the high probability of deteriorating cardiogenic shock.

    Anaesthesia support should be requested via:

    • Elective/Semi-Elective case: Cardiac Anaesthesia group, via the CICU consultant
    • Emergency case: General Anaesthesia group (Bleep 3036), or Cardiogenic Shock Team (CST) activation via 2222 if patient meets calling criteria
  6. ICU Bed Planning

    If the Impella is explanted successfully at the end of the procedure and there are no signs of shock (see Appendix 1 – BHI Ventricular Support Algorithm), the patient may be admitted to a cardiac ward or CCU.

    However, if the Impella remains in situ post-procedure, an ICU bed must be arranged in advance.

Location Requirements

Insertion must take place in:

  • Cardiac catheter laboratory (BHI), OR
  • Cardiac theatres

Do not attempt insertion in other locations.

Operator Requirements

Only the following trained personnel may insert Impella catheters:

  • Consultant Interventional Cardiologist (with Impella training)
  • Consultant Cardiac Surgeon (with Impella training)

All operators must have completed:

  • Abiomed online training programme
  • In-person training with Abiomed representative
  • Proctored cases as required

Team Preparation

Ensure the following personnel are available and prepared:

  • Interventional Cardiologist / Cardiac Surgeon
  • Cardiac Physiologist (for AIC setup and monitoring)
  • Anaesthetist (as per point 5 above)
  • Cath lab nursing staff
  • Receiving ICU team (if device remaining in situ)
