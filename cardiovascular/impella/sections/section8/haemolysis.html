Section 8: Haemolysis

Version 3.2 | March 2025 ‚Äì December 2026

Clinical Signs

Clinical signs include:

  • Red or dark brown urine
  • Jaundice
  • Renal failure

Laboratory Investigation

Results in:

  • Raised bilirubin
  • Raised LDH

These should be tested for.

üî¥ Diagnostic Threshold

Check plasma free haemoglobin - clinically significant haemolysis defined as a plasma free Hb >0.5g/L.

Treatment

  • Rule out non-device related causes
  • Check volume status/preload
  • Check Impella position
  • If haemolysis persists despite above measures, device removal will need to be considered
