Investigation and Management of Pulmonary Embolism

Version 4.4 | October 2024 ‚Äì August 2026 | UHBW Trust-wide Guideline

Comprehensive trust-wide clinical guideline for the investigation and management of pulmonary embolism in adult patients, covering ED assessment, inpatient management, risk stratification, and treatment protocols.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Two-level Wells Score and PERC criteria for PE investigation
  • D-dimer testing and age-adjusted interpretation
  • Imaging modality selection (CTPA vs V/Q scanning)
  • Risk stratification: High risk, intermediate risk, and low risk PE
  • Thrombolysis protocols and contraindications
  • Catheter-directed thrombectomy referral criteria
  • Anticoagulation choice: Apixaban, Rivaroxaban, Enoxaparin
  • Outpatient vs inpatient management (sPESI score)
  • Special circumstances: renal impairment, pregnancy, malignancy
  • Follow-up pathways and echocardiography protocols

PE Interactive Management Tool

Interactive Clinical Decision Support Tool

Interactive web-based tool for rapid clinical decision-making in pulmonary embolism management. Designed for use at the bedside in ED and ICU settings.

Interactive Features:

  • Wells Score Calculator: Interactive two-level Wells score with automatic PE probability assessment
  • PERC Criteria: Pulmonary Embolism Rule-Out Criteria checklist
  • sPESI Calculator: Simplified Pulmonary Embolism Severity Index for outpatient suitability
  • Risk Stratification: Automatic classification into high/intermediate/low risk categories
  • Treatment Pathways: Step-by-step management algorithms based on risk level
  • Anticoagulation Dosing: Weight-based dosing calculators for LMWH and DOACs
  • Thrombolysis Decision Support: Contraindication checker and dosing guidance
  • Follow-up Planning: Automated follow-up pathway recommendations
